design: dominant black background interwoven with bright red patterns. there is a diamond shaped pattern meticulously arranged in horizontal rows.
description: Black often represents strength, resilience, and the earth. Red signifies energy, life, sacrifice, and warrior spirit.
The diamond shape is symbolic in many Naga traditions and often represents:
Protection against evil spirits, Eyes watching over the community and Harmony and continuity. it is worn in festival like mungmung, tribal council meeting or marriage and clan ceremonies.
